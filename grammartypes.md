# Spanish Text Conversation Error Guide

## 1. Grammar

### 1a. Verb Conjugation
- Wrong tense or mood (indicative/subjunctive/conditional).
- Wrong person.
- Ser/estar confusion.

### 1b. Agreement
- Gender mismatches (el/la).
- Number mismatches (singular/plural).
- Adjective–noun agreement errors.

### 1c. Pronouns
- Incorrect direct/indirect object use (lo/le/les).
- Missing reflexive pronouns.
- Incorrect placement with infinitives, gerunds, or commands.

### 1d. Prepositions
- Por vs. para mistakes.
- A/en/de confusion.
- Literal translation patterns from English.

---

## 2. Vocabulary

### 2a. Wrong Word Choice
- Using a technically correct word that sounds unnatural.
- English-influenced choices.

### 2b. Missing Words
- Missing articles or prepositions.
- Missing connectors (y, pero, porque, aunque, etc.).

### 2c. False Friends
- Words that look right but aren’t (e.g., *actualmente*, *embarazada*, *sensible*).

---

## 3. Syntax

### 3a. Word Order
- Misplaced adjectives.
- Incorrect placement of object pronouns.
- English-like structures that sound unnatural.

### 3b. Clause Structure
- Unclear or fragmented sentences.
- Poorly connected clauses.
- Incorrect or missing relative clauses.

---

## 4. Morphology

### 4a. Incorrect Form Building
- Wrong plural forms.
- Wrong gender forms.
- Incorrect comparative or superlative forms.
- Irregular stems misused.

---
